Hi, I'm Kristen!

I began piano lessons at age 5, violin at 9, and viola at 10. I have a BA in Music from Brigham Young University. I have enjoyed many different musical experiences over the years, including:

  • Playing in symphony orchestras
  • Teaching orchestra classes at public elementary schools
  • Playing in chamber groups (trios, quartets, quintets, sextets . . . )
  • Fiddling with folk ensembles
  • Playing string bass in a jazz band
  • Singing in community and church choirs
  • Directing a church choir for adults
  • Serving as children's music director at church
  • Accompanying congregational hymns on the organ and piano
  • Teaching early childhood music and movement classes
  • Providing music for weddings and receptions

I feel so fortunate to have had these experiences, and I work hard to help my students enjoy music as much as I do! I have attended workshops through Suzuki and Musikgarten, and I regularly consume books, blogs, and podcasts on innovative music teaching. I have even been known to write, arrange, and record music for the use of my students! I am also a parent of three young children.
