Violin and Viola Lessons

Violins and violas can make beautiful sounds, with some skill . . . and I can help! I have taught violin for over 10 years, and am trained in the Suzuki Method (an innovative way of teaching that helps break complex skills down to bite-sized steps). I love teaching adults as well as children as young as age 4.5. Up to age 9 or 10, a parent should plan to participate in lessons and supervise daily practice. Recitals are held 2-3 times per year.
