Piano lessons: Learning music in black and white!

My piano teaching specialty is working with beginning to elementary-level players, especially children. My students get to play new, fun songs each week, while building their understanding of music theory from the ground up! I love being part of their learning process and helping them enjoy the success that comes from their own efforts. Lessons are typically held weekly at my home, and are individualized so each student learns at his/her own pace. Recitals are held two to three times per year, giving students a chance to develop important performance and ensemble skills and socialize with other students and families.
